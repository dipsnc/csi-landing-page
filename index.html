About Us

CSI DMCE - Student branch, established in the year 2012, at Datta Meghe College of Engineering, Airoli conducts various workshops, seminars, technical competitions and also Technical fest in association with Computer Association for Technological Trends (CATT). The committee continuously works for fostering experiences, teamwork, networking, etc, in our college.

Mission
Empowering students through innovation, collaboration, and technical growth.
Vision
To be a hub of creativity and technology-driven learning.
Events
Engaging workshops, competitions, and seminars to boost real-world skills.
Education
Hands-on learning beyond the classroom through peer and expert sessions.
